algol - direct impact computation.

This program computes critical parameters for direct impact between two white dwarfs. For each primary mass, it prints out the critical donor mass for impact to occur and the critical donor mass at which impact occurs and the donor just sees the impact spot.

m1low---Mass of primary, lower limit, solar masses
m1high---Mass of primary, upper limit, solar masses
nmass---Number of masses
