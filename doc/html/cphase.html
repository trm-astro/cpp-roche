cphase -- contact phase computation

This program computes the phases of third and fourth contacts of a white dwarf in a CV.

q---Mass ratio = M1/M2
i---Inclination, degrees
m1---Mass or primary, solar masses
period---Orbital period, days
ntheta---Number of angles around the star being eclipsed
acc---Accuracy for eclipse computation
