pdwd - direct impact stuff

pdwd plots the stream in a double white dwarf binary.

m1---Mass of accretor, solar masses
m2---Mass of donor, solar masses
type---Type of system: 'cv' ==> a low mass MS donor with R = M in solar units. 'am' ==> white dwarf donor.
device---Plot device
x1---lower x limit of plot (units of a)
x2---upper x limit of plot
y1---lower y limit of plot
y2---upper y limit of plot
width---width of plot inches
aspect---aspect ratio
lwidth---width of plot lines, as a multiple of the default
