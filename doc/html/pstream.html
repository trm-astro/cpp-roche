pstream - direct impact stuff

pstream outputs the stream in a double white dwarf binary.

q---Mass ratiop, = m2/m1
stype---Stream type, v for velocity, p for position
step---step size in units of K1+K2 for velocity, or 'a' for position
npoints---Number of points
